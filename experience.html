ABOUT US

Founded as a passion project in 2023 and officially established as a business in 2024, THE ARCHIVE has grown into a trusted creative brand recognized for its distinctive visual identity, premium-quality work, and commitment to excellence. Every project is approached with creativity, precision, and a passion for delivering exceptional results.

More than simply documenting events, our mission is to redefine the standards of modern photography and videography through intentional storytelling and refined artistry. We strive to create a unique client experience that reflects elegance, authenticity, and professionalism in every frame.

We proudly offer professional photography and videography services for a wide range of occasions, including:

  • Weddings
  • Debuts & Birthday Celebrations
  • Christenings
  • Family Reunions
  • Pre-Graduation & Graduation Shoots
  • Recognition Ceremonies
  • Corporate Events
  • And many more special occasions.

At THE ARCHIVE, every story deserves to be preserved—not just as photographs and videos, but as timeless memories that can be relived and treasured for years to come.
